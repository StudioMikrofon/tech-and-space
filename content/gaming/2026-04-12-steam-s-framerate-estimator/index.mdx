The tool is expected to utilize anonymized framerate data and hardware information shared by Steam users. This development has significant implications for gameplay, as it could help players make informed decisions about game purchases and system upgrades.


The introduction of the Framerate Estimator tool is a direct result of Valve's data sharing program, which was launched in February. This program enables Steam users to share their anonymized framerate and hardware information with Valve, aiming to improve game compatibility and the overall Steam experience. As [The Verge](https://www.theverge.com/) notes, this move demonstrates Valve's commitment to enhancing the gaming experience for its users.
