Square Enix has partnered with Google to bring AI-powered chatbots to Dragon Quest X. The chatbot, named Chatty Slimey, is based on the iconic blue slimes from the Dragon Quest franchise. Players can interact with Chatty Slimey via voice and text, receiving AI-generated voice responses. This integration is made possible by Google's Gemini AI, which is trained on publicly available data.


The announcement was made at a press event over the weekend, and it marks a significant step forward in the use of AI in gaming. According to [GameSpot](https://www.gamespot.com/), the partnership will allow players to receive guidance and advice from Chatty Slimey throughout the game.


The use of AI-powered chatbots in gaming is not new, but the integration of Gemini AI in Dragon Quest X is a notable development. It remains to be seen how players will respond to the new feature, but it has the potential to enhance the gaming experience.
