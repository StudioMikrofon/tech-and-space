Church leaders and conservative academics were among those present, sparking intrigue about the potential implications of such a meeting. The event's secretive nature has raised questions about the motivations behind it, with [The Verge](https://www.theverge.com/) reporting on the mysterious gathering.


The meeting's focus on artificial intelligence suggests a growing concern among leaders about the impact of AI on society. As AI technology continues to advance, it is likely that we will see more discussions about its role in shaping our future.
