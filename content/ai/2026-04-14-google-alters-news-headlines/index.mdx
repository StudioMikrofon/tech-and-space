Google has been caught replacing news headlines using generative AI, according to a report by The Verge. This change affects both the headlines and their meaning, potentially making journalism less trustworthy. The Verge argues that altering headlines reduces the trustworthiness of journalism, especially at a time when powerful institutions are trying to discredit it.


The use of AI in altering news headlines raises questions about the role of technology in journalism. As [The Verge](https://www.theverge.com) notes, many news organizations are struggling to remain operational, and the use of AI could exacerbate this issue. [Google's](https://www.google.com) actions may be seen as an attempt to improve the user experience, but they also highlight the need for greater transparency in the use of AI in journalism.
