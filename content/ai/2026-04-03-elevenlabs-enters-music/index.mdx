ElevenLabs, known for its voice models, has released ElevenMusic, an AI-powered music-generation app. This move suggests ElevenLabs is aiming to expand its offerings beyond voice modeling. Users can create and remix songs using text prompts, a feature that could democratize music production. According to [TechCrunch](https://techcrunch.com/), the app's release indicates a strategic shift for the company.


ElevenMusic's capabilities, while impressive, raise questions about the quality and originality of AI-generated music. As [The Verge](https://www.theverge.com/) notes, AI music generation is not new, but the ease of use and accessibility of ElevenMusic could make it a significant player in the market. The app's success will depend on its ability to produce high-quality music that resonates with listeners.


The release of ElevenMusic also highlights the growing trend of AI-powered creative tools. As [Wired](https://www.wired.com/) reports, AI is being used in various creative fields, from music and art to writing and design. This trend is likely to continue, with AI-powered tools becoming increasingly sophisticated and accessible to a wider audience.
